About Secret Diary

 Secret Diary  is a project I did because I always wanted to write a diary so that one day I would be able to read back how I was, what I did and when I did it. But I never liked writing by hand, so...

This way I can write whenever I want what comes to mind , wherever I am.
 Secret Diary is also safe to use because it requires a log in, protected with advanced technology to keep your password safe. Safety is my second name.

Now what are you waiting for, go write whatever comes to mind right now before it's too late! Remeber, one day you will regret not writing your diary, and we don't want that!
